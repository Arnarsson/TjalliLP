WHY WE'RE BUILDING THIS

The people making the biggest decisions are drowning in the smallest tasks.

Every founder, executive, and decision-maker we know runs on the same broken system: ten inboxes, three calendars, a hundred browser tabs, and a brain that never fully switches off.

We asked ourselves a simple question: what if you had a private intelligence that knew your world, ran on your hardware, and worked for you alone? Not another SaaS platform. Not another subscription that owns your data. Something fundamentally different.

HEKLA is that answer. Built in Copenhagen. Encrypted by design. Configured one-to-one. She learns your priorities, your people, and your patterns — and she never forgets.
